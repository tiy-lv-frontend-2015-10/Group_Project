The best chicken I've ever tasted...

Private Jim Robinson, a leiutenant in charge of kitchen duty for the United States Marine Corps was constantly under pressure from his superiors when he decided to throw the military's cookbook away and give our soldiers gourmet soulful recipes that will satisfy even the pickiest pallate. He had one supporter who he could confide in... Colonel Major Jack Ryans. He saw his creative talent and encouraged him to study at Flambaye Flamage, an exquisite cooking school known for their brutal selection process. Five years later with his personal savings he and his wife opened JTO. A culinary fusion of international cuisine along with sourthern comforts from his native home of Kentwood, Louisiana.
